About ARSquare Tech LLC

ARSquare Tech Consulting is close to 2 decade in the business of Executive search in Bangalore. Our knowledge of the market and access to qualified talent is unmatched in the industry. Mindsoft country reach ensures our client's needs are served anywhere they conduct business. Each office has specific expertise driven to serve.

  • ARSquare Tech has handled retained search at an international level
